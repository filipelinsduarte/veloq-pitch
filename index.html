Quick Win

Schema markup is
generic across the site.

What's there now

❌
WebPage — on every product pageThe most basic schema type. Tells Google almost nothing about what Veloq does.
❌
No BreadcrumbListGoogle can't display clean URL paths in search results.
❌
No FAQPage schemaMissed opportunity for accordion-style rich results in SERPs.

What it should be

✅
SoftwareApplicationDeclares Veloq as a B2B platform with category, features, and pricing signals.
✅
BreadcrumbList on all pagesEnhances how Veloq appears in Google — cleaner, more professional listings.
✅
FAQPage on product pagesFAQ sections become rich result accordions — more SERP real estate.
Why it matters: Schema doesn't directly boost rankings — but it changes how Veloq looks in results. Richer listings get more clicks, even at the same position.
