Quick Win

Schema markup is
generic across the site.

❌ What's there now
🔴
WebPage on every product pageThe most basic type — tells Google nothing about what Veloq does or sells.
🔴
No BreadcrumbListGoogle can't display clean URL paths in search results.
🔴
No FAQPage schemaMissing the chance for accordion-style rich results in SERPs.
✅ What it should be
🟢
SoftwareApplicationDeclares Veloq as a B2B platform — category, features, and pricing signals.
🟢
BreadcrumbList on all pagesCleaner, more professional listings in Google results.
🟢
FAQPage on product pagesFAQ sections become rich result accordions — more SERP real estate.
Why it matters: Schema changes how Veloq looks in results. Richer listings get more clicks even at the same position — and it costs very little to implement.
